The Rain is Recurring

For my final project I decided to extend my original sketch I did on depicting the concepts of male and female rain. I really enjoyed working on that project and I wanted to make it a piece that was more informing for the viewers. To do this I extended the sketch by adding more of a conceptual framework to my art. Instead of just a static sketch, I build a site around it. On my site the viewer can access the stories of rain, which inspired my animation piece.
